I made a piece of live-coded music made up of 11 loops that I enabled, disabled and altered on the fly. One set of visuals uses abstract blob forms that move smoothly but abruptly cut to new forms and shapes. The other is an image of a stained glass window with various effects that mirror the stained glass look.

One sample is the sound of loose coins slowed down and filtered to create an interesting sound that provides an interesting layer in the background. The coloured blogs are created with a noise pattern filtered to create sharp edges, modulated to move randomly, then pixelated to a 2x2 grid.
